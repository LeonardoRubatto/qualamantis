Where to start

The diagnostic first —
how most engagements begin

Organisations that come to us rarely begin by commissioning a full transformation program. They begin with a question. We offer three diagnostic entry points, run independently or combined — each fixed in scope and timeline, each a complete deliverable in itself.

Quality & EHS Rapid Assessment

2–5 days on-site plus document review. A prioritised gap map, a maturity rating, and recommended actions with effort and impact estimates.

Cost of Non-Quality (CoNQ) Scan

2–3 weeks with a small cross-functional team. A financial picture of what poor quality is costing the business, and where the highest-value opportunities lie.

Sustainability & EHS Maturity Review

A structured assessment of strategy, EHS systems and ESG data readiness against CSRD and SBTi alignment — with a prioritised roadmap for the next 12–24 months.

Fixed scope and timeline. Senior-led. Honest findings — including where you are already performing well. No lock-in: every diagnostic is priced and delivered as a standalone service.
