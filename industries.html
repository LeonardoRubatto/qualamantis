Sector Spotlight

Rolling Bearings — a deep industrial specialisation

Within the broader industrial landscape, Qualamantis has developed a recognised depth of expertise in the rolling bearing industry. This is not a niche we entered by accident — it reflects years of senior engagement with major bearing manufacturers and their supply chains, covering the full scope of quality management, reliability engineering, warranty analysis, manufacturing process development and product design.

Why bearings are different

One component,
a system-wide consequence

Rolling bearings sit at the intersection of materials science, precision manufacturing, tribology and application engineering — critical components in virtually every rotating machine, from automotive drivetrains to wind turbine gearboxes to high-speed rail axles.

A bearing that fails rarely fails alone: it brings down the system around it, with consequences in warranty, safety and reputation that propagate far beyond the component itself.

Getting bearings right demands both technical precision and systemic quality discipline — from raw material selection and heat treatment control through to application design, installation and in-service monitoring. This is where our experience is concentrated.

What we bring

To bearing manufacturers
and their customers

Quality Management Systems

Design and deployment of QMS aligned to IATF 16949, ISO 9001, and sector-specific customer requirements.

Manufacturing Process Development

Particular depth in Heat Treatment and Grinding — the two processes most critical to bearing fatigue life and dimensional accuracy.

Reliability Engineering

Weibull analysis, accelerated life testing, warranty prediction and failure mode characterisation.

Warranty & Field Problem Resolution

Statistical modelling to narrow problem populations and support targeted, defensible corrective actions.

Supplier Qualification & Development

For materials, subcomponents and process inputs specific to bearing manufacture.

Design Review & Application Engineering

Ensuring bearing selection and integration are optimised for the operating environment.

Our bearing expertise extends from the component level — failure modes, fatigue life, contamination analysis, warranty prediction — to the system level: application and design review, supplier qualification, and manufacturing process development. This depth means bearing manufacturers, their customers, and their supply chain partners can engage Qualamantis with confidence: we understand the technical language, the failure mechanisms, the certification standards, and the commercial pressures specific to this sector.
