The Program

One program, three moves that compound

Diagnose → Build → Capture value. Not a report to file away — a transformation with clear owners, KPIs, and your own people trained to carry it on.

01
Gap Assessment
+

Together with your leadership we run a company-level gap and SWOT analysis — covering business performance and product cost as two independent lenses. One honest picture before you spend a euro on change.

  • Gap & SWOT analysisStrategic strengths, weaknesses and blind spots.
  • Business process assessmentWhether your core processes deliver on performance and cost.
  • Hidden-factor assessmentThe culture and behaviours that shape real outcomes.
  • Lean & digital maturityWhere waste, missing data and process gaps slow you down.
02
Action Plan
+

We turn the diagnosis into processes and people that hold — a plan with clear owners and KPIs, built around seven typical workstreams.

  • Critical-to-business processesAPQP, MOC, PPAP, CCM, supplier approval and more.
  • Short-term risk managementContain today's risks before they reach the customer.
  • Competence developmentClose capability gaps across engineering and operations.
  • Problem solving & continuous improvementZero Defect, DMAIC and Six Sigma.
  • Operational supportHands-on help to keep momentum and embed the change.
03
Cost Improvement Program
+

A stand-alone program to engineer product cost down — across all processes, with or without a performance problem. Run by a co-located, cross-functional team with a mandate to act.

  • Product specificationsPrioritize what customers truly value.
  • Sourcing & priceClean-sheet target costing, part by part.
  • Technical solutionsTeardown-driven redesign of product and process.
  • Cost of Non-Quality as a diagnosticLosses quantified and fed back as improvement targets.
In parallel — once the plan is set, digitalisation and cost reduction run together, and each cycle resets the baseline higher.
